About Patria Today

European news, clearly told.

What we do

Patria Today covers European current affairs: decisions in Brussels, events across the continent, and the moments where Europe meets the world. We report what happened, who was involved, and why it matters — without the spin.

Our standards

Every article is built from sourced, verifiable reporting. Where facts are uncertain, we say so. Where claims are disputed, we name who disputes them. We do not publish what we cannot source.

Pro-European, not partisan

Patria Today is pro-European in the sense that we believe the European project is worth covering carefully and honestly. We are not a house organ for any institution, party, or ideological agenda. Our job is to report, not to advocate.

Part of Patria

Patria Today is part of the Patria editorial family — an independent European intelligence operation covering EU law, policy, and politics. Where Patria provides depth and analysis, Patria Today covers the news as it breaks.
